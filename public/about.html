Our Mission

Digitalizing the Dying Art of Traditional Filipino Weaving

HABI-LIN is a bridge between ancestral wisdom and modern technology, designed to preserve the intricate patterns of the Philippines for the digital age.

The Problem

Traditional handloom weaving is a labor-intensive art form that is slowly disappearing as younger generations move toward digital careers. The complex patterns often exist only in the memory of master weavers.

The Solution

By using 3D modeling and data signal processing, HABI-LIN captures the movement of the loom, allowing weavers to create, store, and share digital pattern drafts globally.
