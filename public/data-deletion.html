User Data Deletion Instructions

If you logged into HABI-LIN using Facebook and wish to delete your account and all associated weave patterns, you can remove our app's access by following these steps:

  • Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  • Click Settings.
  • Look for Apps and Websites to see all the apps linked to your Facebook.
  • Search for HABI-LIN in the search bar.
  • Click Remove to revoke access and request data deletion.

Alternatively, you can request the immediate manual deletion of your account from our database by contacting the developer directly.
